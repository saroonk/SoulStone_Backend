Common Questions

How do I know this gemstone is natural?

Every stone ships with an independent gemmological laboratory report confirming it is a natural stone, with any treatment disclosed in full — never sold as natural if it is synthetic or simulated.

Does it include certification?

Yes. A laboratory certificate is included with every purchase at no extra cost, detailing weight, measurements, colour, and treatment.

How should I wear this gemstone?

This stone ships loose so it can be set by a jeweller of your choosing. See the Description & Benefits tab above for traditional guidance on finger, metal, and day.

Can I consult before purchasing?

Yes. Our gem advisors are available over WhatsApp to help you confirm carat, quality, and suitability before you buy — with no pressure to purchase.

How should I care for the gemstone?

Clean gently with lukewarm water and a soft brush, avoid harsh chemicals, and store separately from harder gems. Full guidance is in the Care Instructions heading of the Description & Benefits tab above.
