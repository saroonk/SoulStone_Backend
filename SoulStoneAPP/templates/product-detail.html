About This Gemstone

This Burmese Ruby is a loose, unset natural corundum prized for its deep, saturated red and fine clarity. Each stone in this listing is individually inspected before it is offered, weighed, and matched to its laboratory report.

Rubies form deep within marble deposits under immense heat and pressure, a process spanning millions of years. Trace amounts of chromium give the stone its characteristic red, with the finest examples showing the glow Mogok stones are historically known for.
